# 01 — Hero Slider (Home page top banner)

3 rotating slides. Each needs a **Desktop (1920×820)** and a **Mobile (1080×1400)** version.
Prepend the **GLOBAL STYLE** (see main README) to every prompt below.

The site adds the headline/buttons on top, so **leave clean negative space** (desktop: left side;
mobile: top third). Slides should feel premium and cinematic — dark, warm tones.

---

## Slide 1 — "Train Beyond Limits"
**Theme:** flagship hero — strength + yoga together in a luxurious dark studio.

**🖥️ Desktop** — size **1920 × 820** — save as `slide-1-train-beyond-limits/desktop/slide-1-desktop.jpg`
> [GLOBAL STYLE] Wide cinematic banner of **[MY CLIENT]** standing confidently in a high‑end dark
> home‑gym & yoga studio — warm wood panels, soft greenery, a large round window with diffused
> light. Around them, premium gear arranged tastefully: a rolled yoga mat, water bottle, yoga
> blocks, a wooden yoga wheel, and a rack of dark dumbbells in the background. The client is on the
> **right third**, looking toward camera, calm and powerful; the **left third is open empty space**
> (for the headline). Moody warm‑dark lighting, shallow depth of field, ultra‑premium. Landscape 21:9 feel.

**📱 Mobile** — size **1080 × 1400** — save as `slide-1-train-beyond-limits/mobile/slide-1-mobile.jpg`
> [GLOBAL STYLE] Vertical hero of **[MY CLIENT]** in the same luxurious dark home‑gym & yoga studio
> (warm wood, round window, dark dumbbells, rolled mat, yoga wheel, water bottle). Client framed
> **lower‑centre, full or three‑quarter body**, with **clear empty headroom at the top** for the
> headline. Calm, powerful, premium. Warm‑dark cinematic lighting, portrait composition.
