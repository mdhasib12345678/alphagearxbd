# 04 — Product Images (14 products)

Each product image is **square**. Generate **once** and export **two sizes**:
- **Desktop 1200×1200** → `…/desktop/<slug>-desktop.jpg`
- **Mobile 800×800** → `…/mobile/<slug>-mobile.jpg` (same shot, smaller/lighter file)

For each product you get **two prompt options — pick one:**
- **A) Lifestyle (with your client)** — your client using/holding the product (as you asked).
- **B) Clean product‑only** — a crisp studio shot of just the product (reads clearer in the small
  grid; great for the shop thumbnails). You can mix: e.g. clean for the grid, lifestyle as a 2nd
  gallery image on the product page.

Prepend the **GLOBAL STYLE** to whichever you pick. Centre the product, square composition.
